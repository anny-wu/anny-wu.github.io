About Me

I am a junior at the University of Michigan intending to study Computer Science at the College of Engineering, and I am passionate about languages starting at a young age. Although the definition of the word "languages" was narrow for me at the beginning as I only think about spoken languages, this definition continued to grow and eventually became a part of everything I encountered. From a stripe of color on a piece of artwork to a line of code on the screen of a computer, something has been communicated through the power of languages. Therefore, I wanted to find a place in the computer software industry where art, computer, and spoken languages meet.
